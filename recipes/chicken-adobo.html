Filipino Chicken Adobo

Prep: 15 mins Cook: 45 mins Serves: 4 4.8 (156 reviews)

  • Email

Chicken Adobo is a classic Filipino dish and is often considered the unofficial national dish of the Philippines. This savory and slightly tangy dish features chicken braised in vinegar, soy sauce, garlic, and black peppercorns.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs chicken, cut into serving pieces
  • 1/2 cup white vinegar
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1 whole garlic, crushed
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 tablespoon whole black peppercorns
  • 1/4 cup cooking oil
  • 1 cup water
  • Salt to taste

Nutrition Facts

Calories 385
Protein 28g
Carbs 6g
Fat 24g

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine chicken, soy sauce, and vinegar. Marinate for at least 30 minutes.

  2. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Remove chicken from marinade (reserve marinade) and cook until browned on all sides, about 5 minutes per side.

  3. Add crushed garlic and cook until fragrant, about 1 minute.

  4. Pour in reserved marinade, water, bay leaves, and peppercorns. Bring to a boil.

  5. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer for 30 minutes or until chicken is tender.

  6. Uncover and continue cooking until sauce is reduced and thickened, about 10 minutes.

  7. Season with salt to taste. Serve hot with steamed rice.

Recipe Notes

  • For best results, marinate the chicken overnight.
  • You can use either white or apple cider vinegar.
  • Some regions add a little sugar to balance the flavors.
  • Leftovers taste even better the next day!
