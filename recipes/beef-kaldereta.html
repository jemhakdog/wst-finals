Beef Kaldereta

Prep: 30 mins Cook: 2 hours Serves: 8 4.9 (168 reviews)

  • Email

Beef Kaldereta is a hearty Filipino stew featuring tender beef chunks, liver spread, and mixed vegetables in a rich tomato sauce. This festive dish is perfect for special occasions and family gatherings.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs beef chuck, cubed
  • 3 potatoes, cubed
  • 2 carrots, cubed
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup green olives
  • 1/2 cup liver spread
  • 2 cups tomato sauce
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2-3 bay leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 3 tablespoons cooking oil

Nutrition Facts

Calories 485
Protein 35g
Carbs 28g
Fat 26g

Instructions

  1. Marinate beef in salt and pepper for at least 30 minutes.

  2. Heat oil in a large pot. Sauté garlic and onion until fragrant.

  3. Add beef and cook until browned on all sides.

  4. Add bay leaves and tomato sauce. Simmer for 1-1.5 hours or until beef is tender.

  5. Add potatoes and carrots. Cook for 10 minutes.

  6. Stir in liver spread until well combined.

  7. Add bell peppers and olives. Cook for another 5 minutes.

  8. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve hot with steamed rice.

Recipe Notes

  • For more tender meat, use a pressure cooker (reduces cooking time by half).
  • You can add cheese for a creamier sauce.
  • Some versions include hot chilies for extra heat.
  • Leftovers taste even better the next day!
