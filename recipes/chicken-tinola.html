Chicken Tinola

Prep: 15 mins Cook: 45 mins Serves: 6 4.6 (134 reviews)

  • Email

Chicken Tinola is a hearty Filipino soup made with chicken, green papaya, and moringa leaves in a ginger-flavored broth. This comforting dish is perfect for cold weather and is known for its healing properties.

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken, cut into serving pieces
  • 1 green papaya, cut into wedges
  • 2 bunches moringa leaves
  • 1 thumb-sized ginger, julienned
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 6 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce
  • 8 cups water
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 3 tablespoons cooking oil

Nutrition Facts

Calories 310
Protein 26g
Carbs 15g
Fat 18g

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a large pot. Saut√© garlic, onion, and ginger until fragrant.

  2. Add chicken pieces and cook until lightly browned on all sides.

  3. Pour in fish sauce and cook for 2 minutes.

  4. Add water and bring to a boil. Lower heat and simmer for 20 minutes.

  5. Add green papaya and cook for 5-7 minutes or until tender.

  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  7. Add moringa leaves and cook for another 2 minutes.

  8. Serve hot with steamed rice.

Recipe Notes

  • Chayote can be used instead of green papaya.
  • Spinach can substitute moringa leaves if unavailable.
  • For extra flavor, add lemongrass or chili leaves.
  • Best served during cold weather or when feeling under the weather.
